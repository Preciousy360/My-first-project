Payvice is a Mobile Payment Platform that offers various Value Added Services (VAS), ranging from Airtime purchase, Bill payment, Toll Gate fee, Internet subscription to Cable TV. Services available on the PAYVICE platform includes:
  • Purchase airtime for all networks in Nigeria
  • Subscribe for internet services from popular broadband service providers such as Smile, Spectranet, etc.
  • Pay your water, electricity, movies, toll, subscription renewals and other bills
  • Pay for flight tickets and airline reservations
  • Pay for products on eCommerce sites
